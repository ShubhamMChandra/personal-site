About

I'm a developer and designer who believes the best digital products are the ones you don't notice using.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering and aesthetics. I care deeply about the invisible things: how an interaction feels, the rhythm of a scroll, the moment of delight that makes someone pause and think "this is nice."

Before this, I spent time building products at startups where moving fast meant nothing without building right. Now I help companies and individuals create digital experiences that stand out by being genuinely thoughtful.
